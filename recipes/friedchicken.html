Korean Fried Chicken (Dakgangjeong)

This recipe will aid you in making korean fried chicken at home. It takes an estimated 1 hour 45 minutes to cook.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds of chicken wings
  • Soy sauce
  • Gochujang

Steps

  1. Marinate and dredge the chicken
  2. Fry the chicken 2 times with sauce
