Chocolate Covered Strawberries

This recipe will aid you in making chocolate strawberries at home. It takes an estimated 1 hour 5 minutes to cook.

Ingredients

  • 6 ounces semisweet chocolate, chopped
  • 3 ounces white chocolate, chopped
  • 1 pound strawberries with stems

Steps

  1. Simmer water over medium heat
  2. Set bowls of chocolate over water to melt. Stir til smooth.
  3. Dip Strawberries in chocolate
  4. Set aside till the chocolate sets
